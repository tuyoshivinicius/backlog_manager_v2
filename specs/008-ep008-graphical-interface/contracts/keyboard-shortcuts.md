# Keyboard Shortcuts Contract

## Global Shortcuts (MainWindow)

| Atalho | Acao | Contexto | Requisito |
|--------|------|----------|-----------|
| Ctrl+N | Abrir StoryDialog (modo criacao) | MainWindow focada | FR-120 |
| Ctrl+Shift+A | Executar alocacao automatica | MainWindow focada | FR-125 |
